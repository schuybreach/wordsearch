Wordsearch

This is a tool to search for five letter words! (Primarily for use with Wordle).

Directions (click to expand):

Type any single letter you know in the top five blue boxes. If you don't know the letter, place a * (asterisk / star) in its place. For letters you know are included, but you don't know where, put them in the bottom left green box. And letters that you know are not in the word, in the bottom right red box. Press "search" to search and "reset" to clear the form.

Please Note: The less information you input, the longer it will take to process, please be patient.

If it takes more than 30 seconds, try hitting the "search" button again.

For words that are not five letters, that functionality is still in research and development. Do note that this search will not work on Internet Explorer due to a few "newer features".
